Volunteer in your country

You don’t need to travel across the world to help Ukraine fight against evil. Whether you’re based in America, Australia, or elsewhere, there are opportunities to support both Ukrainian refugees and asylum seekers, as well as Ukrainian Armed Forces.

We have compiled a list of Facebook groups as well as official government organizations that contain valuable information about current volunteer opportunities to help Ukraine while abroad.
